Time Zone

The Egyptian land currently follows Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) time zone plus two hours, during both winter and summer seasons. Egypt observes Eastern European Time all year. Egypt does not utilize daylight saving time DST clock changes. Although until 2015, clocks were advanced for an hour during summertime.
